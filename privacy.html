Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

INTIRA is a productivity and personal finance app designed to help you manage tasks, track debt payoff, and focus on what matters. Your privacy is fundamental to how INTIRA is built.

Data Storage

All your data stays on your device. INTIRA stores your tasks, sprints, blocks, debts, focus sessions, behavioral patterns, and settings locally on your iPhone using on-device storage. No personal data is transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

Behavioral Intelligence

INTIRA tracks how you use the app to improve its recommendations over time. This includes which types of tasks you complete fastest, which days of the week you're most productive, and how you rate your energy after sprints. All behavioral data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted externally. This data is used solely to personalize task scoring, sprint recommendations, and effort estimates within the app.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, or account information. We do not collect task titles, descriptions, or notes. We do not collect debt names, balances, or financial information. We do not collect voice recordings or speech transcripts. We do not track your location. We do not use cookies or tracking pixels. We do not sell, share, or transmit any personal data.

Speech Recognition

INTIRA offers an optional voice capture feature that uses Apple's on-device Speech framework to convert speech to text. When on-device recognition is available, audio is processed entirely on your device and never leaves your iPhone. When on-device recognition is not available, Apple may process audio on their servers subject to Apple's own privacy policy. INTIRA does not store, transmit, or retain any audio recordings.

Microphone Access

INTIRA requests microphone access solely for the voice capture feature. The microphone is only active when you explicitly tap the microphone button. Audio is used exclusively for real-time speech-to-text conversion and is not recorded or stored.

In-App Purchases

INTIRA offers optional Pro subscriptions and a lifetime purchase processed through Apple's App Store. Payment is handled entirely by Apple. INTIRA does not collect or store payment information, credit card numbers, or Apple ID credentials.

Widgets and Live Activities

INTIRA may display task and sprint information on your Home Screen via widgets and Lock Screen via Live Activities. This data is read from a shared on-device container and is never transmitted externally.

Analytics

INTIRA may collect anonymous, aggregated usage analytics such as which features are used and how often to improve the app. This data contains no personally identifiable information and cannot be linked to individual users. You can opt out of analytics in Settings.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Factory Reset" option in Settings. This permanently removes all tasks, sprints, blocks, debts, focus history, and settings from your device. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.

Children's Privacy

INTIRA does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation and stores no personal data on external servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of INTIRA after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@intiraapp.com
