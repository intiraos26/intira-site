Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

THE SHORT VERSION

INTIRA keeps your data on your device and, when you're signed into iCloud, in your own private iCloud account. There are no INTIRA accounts and no INTIRA servers. We never see your tasks, debts, or notes — and we never sell or share them.

Overview

INTIRA is a productivity and personal finance app that helps you manage tasks, run focused sprints, capture ideas, and track debt payoff. Privacy isn't an add-on here — the app is built to work entirely on your device, without accounts or a backend. This policy explains exactly what that means.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on your device and, when you're signed into iCloud, in your private iCloud account. INTIRA stores your tasks, sprints, blocks, debts, focus sessions, usage patterns, and settings on your iPhone. If you're signed into iCloud, this data also syncs to your own private iCloud account so it stays current across your devices and can be restored when you reinstall or set up a new device. INTIRA Labs does not operate its own servers and never transmits your data to us or to any other third party.

iCloud Sync & Backup

INTIRA uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data through your private iCloud database. A few specifics about how this works:

  • Your data lives in your own iCloud account, tied to your Apple ID — not on servers operated by INTIRA Labs.
  • INTIRA Labs cannot access, read, or view the contents of your private iCloud data. Only you, through your signed-in devices, can.
  • Sync is automatic when you're signed into iCloud. If you're not signed in, INTIRA works fully offline using on-device storage only.
  • Apple stores and processes this data as part of iCloud, under Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Your data is never shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or any other third party.

No Account Required

INTIRA does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address, name, or any other identifying information to use the app. There is no login because there is nothing to log in to — the app runs locally.

On-Device Personalization

INTIRA's Signal Engine scores your tasks to help surface what matters most, and it adapts over time based on how you use the app — for example, which kinds of tasks you tend to complete and which days you tend to be most active. All of this is computed on your device. These patterns are stored alongside the rest of your data (on-device, and in your private iCloud account when you're signed in) and are used only to personalize task scoring and recommendations inside the app. They are never transmitted to INTIRA Labs or anyone else.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not collect your task titles, descriptions, or notes.
  • We do not collect your debt names, balances, or any financial information.
  • We do not collect voice recordings or speech transcripts.
  • We do not collect or track your location.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers to track you across other apps or websites, and we do not serve ads.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

INTIRA relies on Apple's built-in App Analytics, which gives developers anonymous, aggregated information — such as how often the app is launched or how many users are on a given version — through App Store Connect. This data is collected and anonymized by Apple, contains no information you enter into the app, and cannot be tied back to you as an individual. You control whether your device contributes this data in Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements, and it's governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

As INTIRA grows, we may introduce additional privacy-respecting analytics or crash-reporting tools to understand how features are used and to fix problems faster. If we do, we will only ever collect anonymous, aggregated usage and diagnostic data — never the contents of your tasks, debts, or notes — and we will update this policy to name those tools before they are enabled.

Voice Capture & Speech Recognition

INTIRA offers an optional Voice Capture feature that uses Apple's built-in Speech framework to turn what you say into a task. When on-device recognition is available, your audio is processed entirely on your iPhone and never leaves the device. In cases where on-device recognition is unavailable, Apple may process the audio on its servers under Apple's own privacy policy. INTIRA does not store, transmit, or retain any audio recordings or transcripts.

Microphone Access

INTIRA requests microphone access only for Voice Capture. The microphone is active only while you are actively recording a task with that feature — it is never listening in the background. Audio is used solely for real-time speech-to-text and is not saved.

In-App Purchases

INTIRA offers an optional Pro subscription processed entirely through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all billing. INTIRA Labs does not collect or store your payment details, credit card numbers, or Apple ID credentials. Apple may share limited, aggregated subscription information with developers; that data is governed by Apple's terms and contains no information you enter into the app.

Widgets & Live Activities

INTIRA can display task and sprint information on your Home Screen (widgets) and Lock Screen (Live Activities). This information is read from a secure, on-device container shared between the app and its widgets. It is never transmitted off your device.

Data Deletion

You are always in control of your data. You can delete everything at any time using the Factory Reset option in Settings, which permanently removes all tasks, sprints, blocks, debts, focus history, and settings. Because your data also syncs to your private iCloud account when you're signed in, note that uninstalling the app does not by itself erase the iCloud copy — that copy remains so your data can be restored when you reinstall or set up a new device. To remove the iCloud copy as well, run Factory Reset before uninstalling, or remove INTIRA's data from iCloud in your device's iCloud settings.

Children's Privacy

INTIRA does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app requires no account and collects no personal information from any user, including children. Any data syncs only to the user's own private iCloud account — never to servers operated by INTIRA Labs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time — for example, if we introduce additional analytics tools as described above. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of INTIRA after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your privacy, please reach out through our Contact page, or email us directly at privacy@intira.io. We're happy to help.
