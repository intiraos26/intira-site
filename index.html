Your focus is the filter. The Signal rises.

Every task you add gets scored automatically. Deadlines, priority, keywords, and context decide what rises to the top as Signal — and what settles into Noise. You just execute.

Signal

The tasks worth your attention right now. The engine weighs deadlines, priority, and context, then brings the right work to the top — so the next move is always obvious.

Noise

Everything else — captured, organized, and ready for later. As deadlines approach and your priorities shift, today's Noise can rise into tomorrow's Signal. Nothing gets lost.

THE NAME, AND THE WHY

Intentional Navigation Through Intelligence, Reasoning & Action.

Most days don't fall apart because we're lazy. They fall apart because everything arrives at once — work, errands, bills, the people who need us — and it all feels equally urgent. The hardest part isn't doing the work. It's deciding what to do first.

I built INTIRA because I wanted a system that could look at everything on my plate and quietly show me where to start. Not another list that grows until it's overwhelming, but something that reasons about what matters and lets me act with intention. Add the task, let the Signal Engine sort it, and trust that the top of the list is the right place to begin.

That's the whole philosophy, in one word: move through your days with intention, guided by a little intelligence — and spend your energy on action, not organization.

Eve Hyppolite
Founder, INTIRA Labs

The Signal Engine

Every task you capture is scored automatically — using deadlines, priority, the words you write, and the context around each task. Tasks due soon are surfaced before they slip, and an adaptive threshold keeps the split meaningful so everything doesn't become "urgent." The more you complete, the better it understands the kinds of work you actually follow through on.

Intelligent Prioritization

Debt Snowball Tracker

Track every debt in a progress ring you can tap to flip between "paid off" and "remaining." The snowball method shows you exactly which debt to crush next — attack the smallest first, and each one you clear frees up cash for the next. Log payments, watch the ring fill, and see your projected debt-free date move closer.

Financial Momentum

One focused system. Four ways to work.

Voice Capture

Speak a task naturally and INTIRA detects the title, due date, tags, and priority — ready to save in seconds.

Sprint Mode

Pick your top Signal tasks, set a focus window, and lock in. Distractions out, momentum in — until the job is done.

Blocks Canvas

A free-form space for thoughts that aren't tasks yet. Drop them on the board, then convert any block to a task in one tap.

Focus Mode

A per-task Pomodoro timer — 15, 25, or 60 minutes, multi-round, with pause and resume. One task, full attention.

iCloud Sync & Backup

Your tasks, debts, blocks, and progress stay in sync across your iPhone and iPad, and they're safe in your private iCloud — so nothing is lost when you reinstall or set up a new device. Sync is automatic when you're signed into iCloud, stored privately in your own account, never on our servers. Not signed into iCloud? INTIRA works fully offline on your device.
