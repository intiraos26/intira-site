The Signal Engine

Every task gets scored automatically using deadlines, effort, focus history, and adaptive thresholds — plus behavioral intelligence that learns your patterns over time. The engine recognizes action verbs, detects people involved, infers effort from how you phrase things, and gets smarter the more you use it.
